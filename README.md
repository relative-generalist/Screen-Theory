## What is Screen Theory?

Screens are bidirectional faithful mediator interfaces that enable
controlled information exchange between distinct regions or systems.

The **Wholographic Principle** asserts that boundaries between interior
and exterior regions encode the mutual, causally transmissible information
between them — what each can objectively know about the other through
their shared interface. Information flows in both directions: the exterior
has objective information about the interior (standard holography), and
the interior establishes its location by reading the boundary (new).
What-where symmetry: location determines contents and contents determine
location. Both are encoded in the same boundary screen.

**Mass as information generator:** Mass actively generates irreversible
classical information at a rate proportional to its energy content,
driving spacetime curvature and geometric structure. The central equation
is:

    dC/dτ = 2π Mc²/ℏ

An isolated mass M generates information at its Compton rate. The
holographic principle requires the surrounding geometry to expand to
accommodate this information. The Schwarzschild metric emerges from
information accounting alone, without invoking Einstein's field equations.

**Spacetime is accumulated, not given.** The local vacuum exists because
the past light cone of every point is saturated by information flux from
all matter within causal reach. Space is not a pre-existing stage —
it is built incrementally from the information output of everything in
causal reach. This vindicates Mach: both space and inertia are relational,
accumulated quantities.

Many further ramifications across multiple fields of physics are
developed in the paper series below.

## The Paper Series

**Paper I: On the Nature of Mass — Complexity Generation and Spacetime
Curvature** (v0.9)
Derives the exterior Schwarzschild metric from one hypothesis and four
assumptions, without invoking Einstein's field equations. Establishes
α = 2π from consistency with the Bekenstein bound. Includes a
cosmological motivation for holographic saturation via an Olbers-style
integration — connecting to Mach's principle and suggesting that
spacetime itself is accumulated rather than given.

**Paper II: On the Nature of Mass II — Local Constraint Generation and
the Einstein Equations** (v1.0)
Derives the null-null components of Einstein's field equations for
vacuum and pressureless dust from the local information generation rate
density J, using infinitesimal causal diamonds as covariant probes.
The factor 8πG emerges from the Bekenstein pixel area (4G) times the
generation constant (2π) with no free parameters.

**Paper III: Cosmology** (in preparation)
Global information balance. Machian origin of mass-energy. The
cosmological constant reinterpreted. Full Olbers integral for the
concordance cosmology.

**Paper IV: Horizons and Black Holes** (in preparation)
Information export rates at event horizons. The Page curve in
information-theoretic terms. Hawking radiation as slow publication.

## Relation to Prior Work

- **Jacobson (1995):** borrows causal diamonds and the Raychaudhuri
  equation; replaces the Clausius relation and Unruh temperature with
  the information generation hypothesis. No temperature, no equilibrium.
- **Penrose:** R-processes as primitive and real, but causal direction
  reversed. State reduction produces geometry, not the other way around.
- **Bekenstein / 't Hooft / Susskind:** holographic principle as the
  bridge from information to geometry.
- **Sciama (1953):** the Olbers integral for holographic area is
  structurally identical to Sciama's calculation of inertial origin.
  Screen Theory provides the deeper framework his calculation was
  pointing toward.
- **Wheeler:** "it from bit" made precise. Geometry (that) from
  information generation (nat).
